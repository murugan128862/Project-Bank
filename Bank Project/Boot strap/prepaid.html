UBS Pre-Paid Cards

Ubs Bank offers a range of pre-paid cards by which one can gift, reward and motivate either your loved ones or your employees in an easy and convenient way. One can choose between Meal Cards, Gift Cards, Rewards Cards and Smart Pay Card. There are a number of benefits of pre-paid cards which include being able to load any amount between Rs. 500 and 10,000 in the Meal Card, being able to make purchases instantly with Gift Card! The Rewards Card offers flexibility to customize ones programs and the Smart Pay Card offers daily POS transaction limit of Rs. 50,000.

  • Meal Card

    • Purpose

      • Issued by corporate organizations for disbursing Tax free Meal amount

    • Special Feature

      • Works at all food & non alcoholic beverage merchant outlets

    • Additional Benefits

      • Load any amount between Rs.500 and Rs.10, 000

    Apply now
