Benefits of opening an UBS Bank Savings Account
A Savings Bank Account helps you maximise the safety of your hard-earned money, as well as earn a steady interest on the balance maintained. Additionally apt customer service and tools offered by UBS Bank go above and beyond traditional banking needs to give you a delightful banking experience. There are a host of Savings Bank Accounts offered, and you can opt for one that best suit your needs: such as discounts and offers on E-Commerce and shopping for users of the Digital Savings Account, or a Basic Savings Account that grants you a zero-balance feature.
Tips to keep in mind while opening a Savings Account
• Know the type/variant of account you would like to open
• Keep your documents (remittance route in case of initial funding) handy
• Ensure accurate communication details shared
• In case on account opening Via Video KYC please ensure that you have a device that can support a video call and a strong internet connection.
